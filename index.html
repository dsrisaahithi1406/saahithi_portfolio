About Me

Welcome! I'm Saahithi — a curious soul, a technologist by profession, and a lifelong learner by passion. This is a space for me to explore and share my (often half-baked!) ideas, hobbies, and thoughts on everything from books to AI to beautiful sunsets. I believe writing is thinking — messy, magical, and meaningful. So I write to think.

“Writing is thinking. To write well is to think clearly. That’s why it’s so hard.” — David McCullough
